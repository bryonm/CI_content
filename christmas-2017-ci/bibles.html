BIBLES

Many families in developing countries are hungry to learn about Christ’s message of peace and redemption, but their economic chains deny them access to the Holy Scriptures.

A $5 gift can put a Bible in the hands of someone eager to study God’s Word. Imagine the joy of a subsistence-level farmer, who has never owned a book in his life, receiving his very own copy of the New Testament, Psalms and Proverbs in his native language to read and meditate upon with his family.

Now he can turn to the Christmas story year-round to lift his spirits and deepen his faith in times of hardship.

Won’t you give this priceless treasure today?
