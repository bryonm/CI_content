MICROENTERPRISE

With 1.1 million residents infected, Zambia ranks 10th in the world for the number of people living with HIV, and a shocking 27,000 people die annually from AIDS-related deaths.

Kitwe Christian Microenterprise provides supplements and seed funding to economically empower Zambians living with HIV and tuberculosis.

Without food, poor Zambians cannot adhere to lifesaving antiretroviral treatment regimens.

For just $18 you can provide someone living with HIV or active TB food assistance for one month.

Your donation will help them continue treatment and help them start small businesses, ensuring they always have enough to eat.
