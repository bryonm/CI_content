LIFE-GIVING WATER

God is doing a great work among the churches, and you can be a part of it! Cross International has committed to construct aqueducts and wells, and provide water filtration systems in impoverished rural communities around the world.

Now we are asking you to help us fulfill that commitment and impact lives for Christ. A gift to this special cause will improve the health and well-being of thousands of families who suffer from waterborne diseases. Through your support, rural evangelical churches will bring physical and spiritual refreshment to their needy neighbors.

Their compassionate witness will be a light in dark places, pointing the lost to the foot of the cross and giving them an experience of Jesus’ amazing love.

This Christmas, give the gift of water!
