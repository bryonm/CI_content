Teach a Child to Read

At Elohim Christian School, children are spiritually nurtured, well-fed and given a Christian education to guide them through the difficulties of living in extreme poverty.

The poor parents of Arequipa, Peru, desperately want to educate their children, but they simply cannot afford the costs associated with sending a child to the public schools. Elohim Christian School bridges the gap, but it can only do so with our support.

For just $6 you can help provide resources that improve students reading skills.

Help these children attain the materials needed to improve their education and break the cycle of poverty.

Bless a needy Peruvian child with a quality education through your donation today!
