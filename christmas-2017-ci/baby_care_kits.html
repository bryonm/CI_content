BABY CARE KITS

Basic baby supplies are a critical part of a newborns life. The blessing of a new baby can be overwhelming, and even more so for mothers in developing countries.

This Christmas, you can bless a mother and baby in need with a Baby Care Kit. With your donation towards a Baby Care Kit, you can provide a baby in need with:

   ●   Lightweight Cotton T-shirts
   ●   Long or Short-sleeved Gowns/Sleepers
   ●   Flannel or Cotton Blankets
   ●   Flatfold Cloth Diapers
   ●   Jacket, Sweater, Sweatshirt with Hood or Baby Cap
   ●   Socks
   ●   Hand Towel
   ●   Bathing Soap
   ●   Diaper Pins, 2-pack

These kits are easily shipped, need no refrigeration, and can be safely stored. Join us today and be Christ’s hands and feet to a baby in need.
