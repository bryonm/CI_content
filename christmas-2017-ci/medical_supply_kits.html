MEDICINE

Give a Christmas gift that will save a life! These medical kits are essential to the work of Christian doctors and volunteers serving the destitute in developing countries around the world.

Oftentimes, they treat patients in villages so remote that even the most basic supplies – such as bandages or Aspirin – are unavailable to those who desperately need them. In such places, something as simple as an infected cut can be a death sentence.

For just $65 can send a Medical Supply Kit packed with items to treat 50 or more sick and injured patients.

Your gift of a Medical Supply Kit will ensure that health care providers are able to bring relief to children, mothers, fathers, the elderly and other vulnerable individuals suffering from illness and injury.
